BIG ELITE LEAGUE – OFFICIAL TERMS AND CONDITIONS

1. Eligibility: All registered teams must have at least 11 registered players. Team representatives must be 18+ years old. Any false information may lead to disqualification.

2. Code of Conduct: Teams and their members must maintain fair play, respect officials, opponents, and spectators. Unsportsmanlike conduct will result in fines or suspension.

3. Registration Fee: A non-refundable registration fee of $250 is required within 7 days after approval. Failure to pay results in forfeiture of spot.

4. Match Scheduling: The league reserves the right to set fixtures. Postponements are only allowed under extreme weather or mutual agreement with league committee.

5. Insurance & Liability: All participants play at their own risk. The Big Elite League is not liable for injuries, loss, or theft of personal property during events.

6. Roster Changes: Teams may add or remove players before the deadline. Each new player must be registered with full name & ID.

7. Disciplinary Actions: Red cards, violent conduct, or abusive language may lead to player/team suspension or expulsion from the league without refund.

8. Image & Media: By registering, teams grant permission to use photographs, video footage, and team names for promotional purposes.

9. Amendments: Big Elite League reserves the right to update rules at any time with fair notice to registered teams.
